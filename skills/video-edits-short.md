Editing preferences for **short-form** videos (30–60 seconds, vertical). These are the producer's standing corrections — the things they have had to ask for, pass after pass, on shorts the editor got almost right. Follow them on the first cut so they don't have to be asked for again.

This document is written by the producer, and grown by the app: when they finish editing a short and press "Update the short-form video skill with these changes", the edits they requested on that video are folded in here. It should read as a set of rules, not as a diary of one video.

## Standing rules

- **Open on the payoff.** A short has no runway. The first frame is the hook; setup comes after it, or not at all.
- **Cut tight.** Dead air, throat-clearing, "so basically", and the pause before a punchline all go. If a beat isn't the hook, the setup, or the payoff, it isn't in the video.
- **One idea.** A short that tries to make two points makes neither.
- **Hold the frame.** Vertical 9:16, subject centred and large. Keep the top and bottom eighths clear of anything that must be read — the platform's own UI sits there.
- **Captions are assumed.** Shorts are watched muted.
